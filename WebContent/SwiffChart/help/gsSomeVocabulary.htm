Some Vocabulary

In order to correctly manipulate Swiff Chart Generator, you need to know what is a Chart Title, a Data Series, the Categories, etc.


In the chart display above:

  1. The Chart Title is Movie Revenues per Week.
  2. The chart has 3 Series, named:
    • Harry Potter
    • Monsters, Inc.
    • Lord of the Rings
  3. Each series has 4 Values.
  4. The chart has 4 Categories, named:
    • Week 1
    • Week 2
    • Week 3
    • Week 4


The data can be displayed as the following table, with series in rows:

      Week 1 Week 2 Week 3 Week 4
    Harry Potter 93.5 58.5 21.0 14.8
    Monsters, Inc. 63.4 46.2 23.0 24.3
    Lord of the Rings 45.3 37.3 23.0 16.2
